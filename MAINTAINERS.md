## Becoming a Maintainer

Maintainer status is granted to trusted contributors who:

- Demonstrate consistent, high-quality contributions  
- Follow the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) and [Contributing Guide](CONTRIBUTING.md)  
- Are active in the Projonexa freelancer or core team network  

Express interest via [Careers](https://projonexa.com/careers) or email the founder.
