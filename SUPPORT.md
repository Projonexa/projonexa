## I found a bug in the website (this repository)

1. Search [existing issues](https://github.com/nikobuddy/projonexa/issues) first  
2. Open a [Bug Report](https://github.com/nikobuddy/projonexa/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml) with reproduction steps  
3. Include browser, device, and page URL if applicable
