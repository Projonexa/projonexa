## Introduction

**Projonexa** is a technology-driven innovation platform that bridges the gap between ideas and execution. We serve students, colleges, startups, researchers, freelancers, and businesses who need expert support to turn concepts into production-ready solutions.

Whether you need a **final year project**, an **AI/ML system**, a **startup MVP**, or **research paper assistance** — Projonexa delivers end-to-end development with industry-level quality.

## About Projonexa

Many students and innovators have groundbreaking ideas but lack:

- Technical expertise  
- Development resources  
- Documentation support  
- Research guidance  
- Time and mentorship  

**Projonexa exists to solve this.**

We provide complete project development services — from mini projects and engineering submissions to enterprise software, IoT systems, and publication-ready research.

## Technology Stack

**Projonexa works across the full technology spectrum — every stack, framework, and platform that college students, universities, and innovators commonly need for academic and real-world projects.**

Whether your syllabus asks for **Java**, **Python**, **React**, **Android**, **IoT**, or **AI/ML** — we match your college requirements and deliver **everything end-to-end**: working source code, SRS & design documents, database setup, deployment, presentation (PPT), demo video, and viva preparation.

### What You Get (Complete Package)

| Deliverable | Included |
|-------------|----------|
| **Source Code** | Clean, documented, submission-ready |
| **Documentation** | SRS, UML, DFD, ER diagrams, user manual |
| **Reports & PPT** | Project report + presentation slides |
| **Database** | Schema, queries, setup guide |
| **Deployment** | Cloud/hosting setup & live demo URL |
| **Viva Support** | Q&A prep and code walkthrough |
