# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Projonexa is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and professional environment for everyone who interacts with our platform — including **students**, **colleges**, **freelancers**, **contributors**, **clients**, and **partners**.

We expect all participants to act with integrity, respect, and collaboration.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, inclusive, and professional in all communications  
- Listen actively and provide constructive feedback  
- Honor deadlines, agreements, and academic integrity guidelines  
- Protect confidential client and student project information  
- Credit collaborators and respect intellectual property  
- Report concerns promptly and in good faith  

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind  
- Plagiarism, academic dishonesty, or misrepresentation of work  
- Sharing private project code, documents, or client data without permission  
- Spam, scams, or misleading service claims  
- Threats, intimidation, or personal attacks  
- Any conduct that violates applicable laws or institutional policies  

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, and discussions  
- Email, social media, and messaging related to Projonexa  
- Client meetings, freelancer collaborations, and community events  
- All official Projonexa platforms and services  

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

1. A private warning and request to correct behavior  
2. Temporary suspension from collaboration or community channels  
3. Permanent removal from the Projonexa network  
4. Referral to academic or legal authorities when appropriate  

Reports are reviewed by the Projonexa leadership team. We aim to respond within **5 business days**.
